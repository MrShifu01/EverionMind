## On-This-Day Resurfacing *(from competitor research)*
Every day, Everion quietly surfaces what you captured on this date last year, two years ago, three years ago. No action required — just a single card in your feed. Day One proved the emotional hit of this is enormous. The "graveyard problem" (capturing but never revisiting) is the #1 unsolved problem in this category.

| Usefulness | Scope Creep | Wow Factor | Unique |
|------------|-------------|------------|--------|
| 8/10       | 2/10        | 8/10       | 5/10   |

---

## Full-Content Web Clip Indexing *(from competitor research)*
When a user saves a link, also save the full text of the page — not just the URL. Fabric.so does this and it's their most-praised feature. Users can search the content of articles even if the original page is deleted or paywalled. Turns "I saved a link" into "I saved knowledge."

| Usefulness | Scope Creep | Wow Factor | Unique |
|------------|-------------|------------|--------|
| 8/10       | 5/10        | 7/10       | 6/10   |

---

## Personal CRM Layer (Rich Contact Objects) *(from competitor research)*
Evolve the existing contact capture type into a first-class object: relationship type, company, last interaction date, linked notes, follow-up reminder. Capacities and Tana gesture toward this but both require high setup overhead. No mainstream second-brain app does lightweight personal CRM well. Everion already has contacts — this is the next layer.

| Usefulness | Scope Creep | Wow Factor | Unique |
|------------|-------------|------------|--------|
| 7/10       | 6/10        | 6/10       | 7/10   |

---

## Proactive AI Push — "Your AI Noticed Something" *(from competitor research)*
The AI scans your memory store nightly and pushes a small number of high-value observations: "You have 5 notes about starting a business but no action item." "You haven't followed up with this contact in 6 months." "You've captured this same idea 3 times — maybe it's worth acting on." Tana is moving this direction with supertag agents but it's not consumer-accessible. Moves Everion from reactive retrieval to genuinely intelligent companion.

| Usefulness | Scope Creep | Wow Factor | Unique |
|------------|-------------|------------|--------|
| 8/10       | 4/10        | 9/10       | 8/10   |
